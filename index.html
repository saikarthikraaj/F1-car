F1 Car Evolution

1950s

Front-engined pioneers

Cars with narrow tires, drum brakes, and minimal safety features. Power outputs around 250hp.

1970s

Aerodynamic revolution

Introduction of wings and ground effects. Power increased to over 500hp.

1980s

Turbo era

Turbocharged engines producing over 1000hp. Carbon fiber monocoques introduced.

2000s

Technological advancement

Electronics, traction control, and advanced aerodynamics dominated car design.

2020s

Hybrid technology

Complex hybrid power units, focus on sustainability, and safety innovations like the halo.
